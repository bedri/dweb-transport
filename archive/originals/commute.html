Dear Internet Archive Supporter,

I ask only once a year: please help the Internet Archive today. We’re an independent, non-profit website that the entire world depends on. Most can’t afford to donate, but we hope you can. If everyone chips in $50, we can keep this going for free. For the price of a couple books, we can share those books online forever. When I started this, people called me crazy. Collect web pages? commute

by Tracey Jaquith

Publication date 2005
Usage http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-nd/2.0/
Topics time-lapse, driving
Publisher PoohBot Pictures

Time-lapse recording of drive from Lafayette, CA to Internet Archive in the Presidio, San Francisco, CA.

About 6500 JPEGs were shot continuously at 2.4fps using the small (but mighty!) Canon SD-300 (and a tripod).
I sampled 1000 images at equal intervals and created this motion-JPEG at 10fps using Cygwin mencoder.

The "Motion JPEG" file is 640x480, 10fps.

Expensive professional camera rig 8-):
